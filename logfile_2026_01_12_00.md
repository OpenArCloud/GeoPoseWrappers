### Cesium GeoPose Converter Library
**File:** `wrappers/cesium/GeoPoseConverter.ts`
- `getCameraGeoPose(camera)` - Get camera pose as GeoPoseBQ

- Handles ENU ↔ ECEF quaternion transformations
- HPR ↔ Quaternion conversion utilities

**Location:** `sandbox/` folder
